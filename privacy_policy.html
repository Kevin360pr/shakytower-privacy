Privacy Policy

Shaky Tower — Last updated: April 2026

Thank you for playing Shaky Tower. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Do Not Collect

Shaky Tower does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. We do not require account registration, and we do not have access to your name, email address, location, or any other personal data.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is saved locally on your device only, using Apple's standard UserDefaults storage. It never leaves your device and is not accessible to us:

  • Your high score and last score
  • Shaky Coins balance
  • Unlocked skins and backgrounds
  • Sound and haptics preferences

In-App Purchases

Shaky Tower offers optional in-app purchases (Shaky Coins packs) processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not handle, store, or have access to your payment information. All transactions are managed by Apple in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

Advertising

Shaky Tower displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads in accordance with Google's privacy policies. You can learn more about how Google uses this data at policies.google.com/privacy.

We do not sell your personal data to advertisers or any third parties.

Game Center

If you choose to connect with Apple Game Center, your scores may be submitted to Game Center leaderboards. This feature is entirely optional and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Shaky Tower does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store and contains no content inappropriate for children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

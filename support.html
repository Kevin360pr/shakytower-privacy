Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn Shaky Coins?

You earn coins by playing — each block you place gives you coins, and perfect placements give bonus coins. You can also watch a short ad for +15 coins, or purchase coin packs in the store.

How do I unlock skins and backgrounds?

Open the Store from the main menu. Spend your Shaky Coins to unlock any skin or background you want.

I made a purchase but didn't receive my coins.

Open the Store → tap "Buy Coins" → tap "Restore Purchases". If the issue persists, contact us at the email below.

Does the game require internet?

No — you can play offline. Internet is only needed for ads, leaderboards, and in-app purchases.

How do I turn off ads?

Ads only appear voluntarily (Watch Ad for +15 coins) or between games. There is no forced ad interruption during gameplay.
